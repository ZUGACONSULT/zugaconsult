About us

Construction projects management and expertise

ZUGA CONSULT is a consultant in construction projects, buildings in particular, based in Morocco and created in mid-2022.

The consultant has a ten year experience in the construction field and has worked mostly in Morocco and also in Nigeria and Turkmenistan. With a significant experience in an international building company followed by Project Management for different types of clients.

  • PROJECT MANAGEMENT

As a consultant to the Project owner or operator, the Project Manager is responsible for overseeing all aspects of the building process. We determine the specifications of the project, negotiate contracts with external vendors to reach profitable agreements, and obtain permits and licenses from appropriate authorities. We also determine resources needed from start to finish with attention to budgetary limitations. We plan and coordinate all construction operations and schedules intermediate phases to ensure deadlines will be met. The Project Manager evaluates progress and prepares detailed reports and ensures adherence to all health and safety standards and report issues.
